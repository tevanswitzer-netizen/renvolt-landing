The Opportunity
===============

Alberta's energy infrastructure is at an inflection point. Government is committing billions to EV infrastructure and clean energy. Industrial customers face rising demand charges and ESG mandates. And within 3-5 years, millions of retired EV batteries will need a second life.

Most companies address one of these markets. Renvolt addresses all three through an integrated platform that compounds value across EV charging, industrial energy storage, and circular battery economy.

The Integration Advantage
=========================

Each pillar is independently profitable. Together, they create compounding value that single-pillar competitors cannot replicate.

Four Synergy Mechanisms
-----------------------

**1. Shared Infrastructure**

* One warehouse, one technical team, one software platform across all three pillars
* 30-40% OpEx reduction vs. operating three separate companies

**2. Cross-Selling**

* EV charging site owners → BaaS prospects (same buyer, proven trust)
* Aging BaaS systems → second-life battery replacements (lower cost, circular)
* 3-4x improvement in customer lifetime value

**3. Data Network Effects**

* Integrated data from 50+ EV sites, 60+ BaaS systems, and 800+ battery units
* Optimization algorithms improve with scale; competitors can't replicate the dataset

**4. Brand Consolidation**

* "Renvolt" = Alberta's integrated energy platform
* Each deployment announcement amplifies brand visibility across all pillars
