**Renvolt — Pilot Site Financial Summary**

Bear Hills Casino & Travel Resort — QE2 Highway, Maskwacis, AB

*First Nation Partnership (Louis Bull Tribe) — 75% ZEVIP Eligible*

How This Site Makes Money
-------------------------

Renvolt buys electricity at wholesale ($0.10/kWh), sells it to EV drivers at $0.50/kWh, and earns additional revenue from demand charge optimization and grid services. Pricing is set below Tesla Supercharger ($0.65–$0.71) and Petro-Canada (~$0.70–$0.94 effective), while leveraging Bear Hills’ monopoly position as the only DC fast charger for 100 km on the QE2.
