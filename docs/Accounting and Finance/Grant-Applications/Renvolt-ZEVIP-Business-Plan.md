### 3.4 How the System Works

The Renvolt battery-integrated charging station operates as follows:

* Off-Peak Charging: The 100 kWh battery charges from the grid during off-peak hours when electricity rates are lowest and grid demand is minimal.
* Peak Demand Shaving: During high-demand periods (multiple vehicles charging simultaneously), the battery supplements grid power, reducing the peak demand drawn from the utility.
* Grid Impact Reduction: By smoothing the load profile, the battery reduces strain on local distribution infrastructure â€” a benefit to the utility and surrounding community.
* Cost Optimization: Demand charge savings of approximately 50% ($6,000â€“$20,000/year per site) ensure the station operates profitably even at early-stage utilization rates, supporting long-term financial sustainability without ongoing subsidies.
