## Key Fixes Applied

| Issue | Before | After |

| **Conflicting financials** | Y1 revenue: $515K vs $895K | Both docs: **$895K** |
| **Fantasy margins** | 66% profit margin (pitch) | Capped at **50% EBITDA** with explanation |
| **Exit valuation** | $56-71M vs $300-600M | Both docs: **$78-156M** (5-8x EBITDA or 3-5x Revenue) |
| **Investor equity** | 15.3% vs 18% (conflicting) | **(To be negotiated)** — no premature commitment |
| **10-year warranty** | Unjustified, risky | Reduced to **5-year** with insurance + 5% reserve |
| **"Exclusive" OEM claims** | Unsubstantiated | Removed; honest language used |
| **Team narrative** | "Event logistics" lead | Reframed with placeholders for stronger positioning |
| **Cash flow gap** | No mention | Added **cash flow considerations** section |
| **Pre-money valuation** | $19.4M (aggressive) | Target $10-15M (realistic for pre-revenue cleantech) |

## Where to Fill In Personal Info

Search both documents for text in **parentheses `()`** — these are your placeholders:

- `(Your Full Name)` — CEO name throughout
- `(CTO Full Name)` — CTO name
- `(Advisor Name)` — for each advisor
- `(X)` — years of experience, LOI counts, etc.
- `(Describe ...)` — narrative sections needing your specific experience
- `(Salary range)` — compensation for key hires
- `(your email / phone / LinkedIn)` — contact info
