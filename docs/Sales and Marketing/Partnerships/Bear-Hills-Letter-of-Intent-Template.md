**RE: Installation of Zero-Cost DC Fast Charging at Bear Hills Casino**

This Letter of Intent (“LOI”) expresses the mutual interest of Renvolt and the Host in the installation, operation, and revenue-sharing of a commercial electric vehicle (EV) fast-charging hub at the Bear Hills Casino property.

The purpose of this LOI is to demonstrate site host support for Renvolt’s application to the federal Zero Emission Vehicle Infrastructure Program (ZEVIP) before the March 20, 2026 deadline. The project intends to utilize the 75% funding tier available to Indigenous-partnered deployments.

**Proposed Terms (Non-Binding):**

1. **Infrastructure:** Renvolt intends to install two (2) 150kW DC Fast Charging dispensers, backed by a 100kWh behind-the-meter Battery Energy Storage System (BESS) designed to strictly limit grid draw to 50kW (preventing utility demand charge spikes for the property).
2. **Zero Capital Cost to Host:** Renvolt will secure the ZEVIP grant funding and bear 100% of all capital costs, including equipment, utility upgrades (if any), permitting, installation, and ongoing maintenance.
3. **Host Obligations:** The Host will provide designated parking spaces and access to existing 208V/600V electrical infrastructure to tie in the 50kW baseline load.
4. **Revenue Share:** The Host will receive a negotiated percentage (estimated at 10-15%) of all gross charging revenue generated by the site, paid out monthly as passive royalty income.
5. **Economic Benefit:** The installation will capture EV traffic traveling the QE2 highway, driving high-income individuals into the casino property for 30–45 minute charging sessions.
6. **Term:** The proposed operating term is ten (10) years with an option to renew.
7. **Insurance:** Renvolt will maintain comprehensive commercial liability insurance covering all installed equipment.

**Status of Document:**
This LOI is non-binding and does not create any legal or financial obligation for either party. It is intended solely to establish mutual interest for the purpose of the ZEVIP grant application. A definitive, binding site host agreement will be negotiated and executed prior to any construction or capital investment.
